🌱 What is this project?

scary-auntie is a community science project that helps children and young people in Alaska record what they know about the plants around them. Every observation is saved with a blockchain record — a special kind of digital fingerprint that makes sure the information can never be changed without leaving a trace.

You can record a plant using its Native Alaskan name, its Latin scientific name, or its English name. All three are important and honored here.

💡 How to contribute

  1. Go outside and find a plant you know.
  2. Take a photo (ask permission if needed!).
  3. Click "Add Observation".
  4. Tell us where you found it, what you call it, and what you know.
  5. A teacher will review and approve your entry.

You must have permission from a parent or guardian to submit.
