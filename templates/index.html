🌱 What is this project?

scary-auntie is a community science project that helps children, young people, and elders record what they know about the plants and the natural world around them. Every observation is saved with a blockchain record — a digital fingerprint that makes sure the information can never be changed without leaving a trace.

You can record knowledge using your Native name, its Latin scientific name, or its English name. All three are important and honored here.

This project is customizable — any group, clan, tribe, village, or school can adapt the wheel to their own languages, territory, and knowledge priorities.

💡 How to contribute

  1. Go outside and observe a plant, animal, weather pattern, or natural feature.
  2. Take a photo (ask permission if needed!).
  3. Click "Add Observation".
  4. Choose a knowledge category, tell us where you found it, what you call it, and what you know.
  5. A teacher will review and approve your entry.

You must have permission from a parent or guardian to submit.
